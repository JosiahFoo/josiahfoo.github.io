About us!

Over 200 years of bread and pastery making experience.

Established in 1812, Our founders have been practising, refining, and evolving the art of makinig bread and pasteries. We pride ourselves in providing the consistent quality bread and allowing our customers to enjoy new innovative pasteries

Well over one million slices a year have been served from our stores collectively.

The integrity of Baker's Delight is on the foundation of the morals of quality products, satisfied customers and consumers, very careful and concerned employees and the dedications to innovative growth and development. We embark on providing the community with fresh and nutritive bake products that it has never witnessed before, while protecting and improving the reputation of bake products and services we provide.

Innovating with the times

Here at Baker's Delight, we also wish to satify the tastes of the new generation, coming up with new and exiciting flavors for our pasteries.
